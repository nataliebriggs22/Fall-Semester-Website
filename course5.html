Course Overview

In Advertising, students are getting an overview of the advertising industry from the perspective of an agency. We are learning about the entire advertising process, including research, creative strategy, execution and media planning. Students will gain insight into the objectives of advertising such as how the business operates and its impact on consumers and culture. By the end of the course, we will understand the role of advertising in domestic and international markets and gain the skills to plan, execute, and evaluate advertising campaigns.
