Course Overview

In Creative Writing, Professor Allnutt focuses on students developing and improving upon their own writing. This class is very writing heavy and writing focused. Students can be expected to respond to multiple readings per week as well as producing two original works per week. The class is centered on specific writing tools such as setting, voice, characterization, sensory detail, and metaphor. At the conclusion of the class each student will have created a personal portfolio consisting of five polished works or prose and poetry.
