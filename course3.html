Research Interests

Doc White's primary research interests include farm management, agricultural financial management, entrepreneurship, and personal financial management.

Publications

He has published many articles relating to agriculture and financial planning, including a yearly newsletter for the Dairy Pipeline.

Service

Doc White is committed to the SmartFarm Innovation Network which created agricultural communication networks throughtout Virginia. They are a response team time-critical incidents and help guide industry decisions. They focus on sustainable farming and doing what's best for the planet and agriculture. He has helped teach students about how to responsibly handle their money and worked with Farm Credit University to help train personnel. He also owns a BBQ business with his brothers, mainly to enjoy food with friends and family.

Education

He received a Bachelors of Science in Agricultural Economics from Virginia Tech, a masters in Agricultural Economics from Ohio State, and a Ph.D from Virginia Tech, with his research focusing on retirement strategies for agricultural producers and small businesses.

Course Overview

In Personal Financial Planning, we are learning about essential skills to manage their finances and setting them up to be in control of their financial future. Doc White makes personal finance individualized and easy to understand. He wants this course to empower students to get ahead of their finances by covering setting life goals, creating a budget, managing credit cards, understanding credit history and scores, and navigating basics of income taxes. This class will help shape students financial future and allow them to learn with ease.
