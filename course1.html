Professor Carlos Evia

Research Interests

Carlos Evia is the Associate Dean for Transdisciplinary Initiatives, Chief Technology Officer, and a professor in the School of Communication. His research interests include intelligent content, content strategy, and DITA (Darwin Information Typing Architecture.) Carlos loves Chappel Roan and fried chicken and considers himself a Swiftie.

Publications

Carlos Evia has written four books, including Creating Intelligent Content with lightweight DITA. He has published many articles on the topic.

Service

He is a member of the Lightweight DITA Subcommittee, Society for Technical Communication, and Virginia Tech Hispanic/Latino Faculty and Staff Caucus.

Education

He got his undergraduate degree in Communication Studies in Merida, Mexico, masters in Computer Assistance at the University Lasalle, and his Ph.D in Technical Communication from Texas Tech.

Course Overview

In Developing Intelligent Content, we are exploring the concept and the significance of intelligent content in technical communication. We have covered different markup languages and content management systems and implemented them in our own websites. The course also encourages the appropriate use of AI such as ChatGPT in creating code and other applications. This class allows students to gain the skills and knowledge to create diverse, intelligent content towards emerging technologies to ensure effective communication solutions.
