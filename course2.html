Course Overview

In Marketing Communications, students will participate in project based learning. This semester we are working with actual clients and creating and implementing an effective, integrated marketing communication plan. Students will act as a marketing firm, collaborating with real-world professional organizations and solve real-time market changes. We are learning to make strategic marketing decisions by working effectively in teams and engaging with business professionals.
